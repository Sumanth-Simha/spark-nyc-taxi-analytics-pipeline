# Phase 8: Building Analytical Data Marts

## Objective

After successfully building the Hive Data Warehouse and registering the cleaned NYC Taxi dataset as an external Hive table, the next objective was to create analytical Data Marts. The goal was to transform the large warehouse table into smaller, business-oriented datasets optimized for reporting, dashboarding, and decision-making.

The warehouse table `fact_taxi_trips` contains 186,394,753 taxi trip records and acts as the single source of truth for all analytical operations.

---

## Why Data Marts Were Created

Querying the entire warehouse table repeatedly for every business question is computationally expensive and inefficient.

Data Marts provide:

* Faster analytical queries.
* Pre-aggregated business insights.
* Reduced computation overhead.
* Simplified dashboard development.
* Subject-specific datasets for decision making.

In enterprise environments, dashboards and BI tools rarely query raw warehouse tables directly. Instead, they interact with curated Data Marts.

---

# Created Data Marts

## 1. mart_yearly_revenue

### Purpose

Designed to analyze long-term business growth and yearly revenue trends.

### Metrics Included

* Total trips per year.
* Total yearly revenue.
* Average trip revenue.
* Average trip distance.

### Business Questions Answered

* How has taxi revenue evolved over time?
* Is trip demand increasing or decreasing?
* How has average customer spending changed over the years?

### Potential Stakeholders

* Executives.
* Financial analysts.
* Business strategy teams.

---

## 2. mart_peak_hours

### Purpose

Created to identify hourly demand patterns and operational peaks.

### Metrics Included

* Pickup hour.
* Total trips.
* Revenue generated per hour.
* Average fare amount.

### Business Questions Answered

* What are the busiest hours of the day?
* During which hours is revenue highest?
* When should additional taxis be deployed?

### Potential Stakeholders

* Fleet management teams.
* Operations managers.
* City transportation authorities.

### Business Questions Answered

### Business Questions Answered

---

## 5. mart_distance_analysis

### Purpose

Created to study customer travel patterns based on trip distance.

### Distance Categories

* Short Trips (<2 miles)
* Medium Trips (2-5 miles)
* Long Trips (5-10 miles)
* Very Long Trips (>10 miles)

### Metrics Included

* Number of trips per distance category.
* Average revenue generated.
* Total revenue generated.

### Business Questions Answered

* What type of trips occur most frequently?

* How does customer travel behavior vary?

### Potential Stakeholders

* Transportation analysts.
* Business strategy teams.
